# Online Delivery Platforms: Sales and Trends (2022-2025)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance and customer behavior across major online delivery platforms (Blinkit, JioMart, and Swiggy Instamart). The dashboard visualizes key metrics to help identify peak revenue seasons, platform popularity, and product quality satisfaction.

## 🔑 Key Insights
*   **Customer Sentiment:** Over 55% of customer feedback is positive, indicating high overall satisfaction.
*   **Top Platform:** **Swiggy Instamart** leads in total orders and is the highest ordering platform according to the trend analysis.
*   **Seasonality:** Revenue peaks significantly in the early months of the year (January - March) before stabilizing.
*   **Product Categories:** Analysis covers a wide range including Beverages, Dairy, Fruits & Vegetables, and Personal Care.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
*   **Microsoft Excel:** Data cleaning, Pivot Tables, and Dashboard creation.
*   **Slicers:** Used for interactive filtering by Product Category, Platform, Feedback type, and Date.

## 📈 Dashboard Features
*   **Product Quality Rating:** Bar chart comparing average vs. current ratings across categories.
*   **Customer Feedback:** Pie chart breakdown of Positive, Neutral, and Negative sentiments.
*   **Revenue Trends:** Line graphs showing "Peak Revenue Seasons" and platform growth.
*   **Platform Comparison:** Horizontal bar charts showing the "Number of Orders Per Platform."
